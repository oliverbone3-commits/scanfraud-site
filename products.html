Why it matters

Measurable impact on your business

Every module is built to move specific metrics — chargeback rates, approval rates, fraud losses, and operational costs.

Lower chargeback rates
Keep chargeback ratios within card network thresholds. Avoid monitoring programs, fines, and the risk of PSP offboarding.
Reduced fraud losses
Catch fraudulent transactions before they clear. Real-time rules, velocity checks, and device fingerprinting reduce fraud losses significantly.
Higher approval rates
Fewer false declines means more completed orders. Smart routing rescues failed payments by cascading to backup providers automatically.
Less operational overhead
Automate fraud detection and dispute workflows. Reduce manual review queues. Free up your team to focus on growth, not firefighting.

02 — ROUTING & CASCADING

Route every payment to the right provider

Define routing rules using static parameters (geography, currency, card type) or dynamic ones (provider performance, fees, limits). When a provider fails, cascading automatically reroutes — no revenue lost, no customer impact.

  • No-code interface — create and update rules in minutes
  • Dynamic routing based on real-time provider metrics
  • Traffic distribution across multiple providers
  • Automatic fallback when a provider is down or at threshold
