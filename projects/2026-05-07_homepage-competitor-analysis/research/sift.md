## Patterns to learn from
- Hero combines an analyst/G2 trust badge eyebrow with a benefit-led, parallel-structure headline (verb-noun-adverb x2).
- Specific named customers and a quantified outcome (85% chargeback drop) inside the feature rows, not separated into a logo bar.
- Interactive lifecycle tabs ("Signup → Post-transaction") let visitors self-select what's relevant.
- Three stat numbers chosen to feel network-scale (1T events, 1.6B identities) — tied to the data network as a moat.
- Resource block leans on the "Agentic AI" trend to feel current.

## Patterns to skip
- Single hero CTA loses the demo path; "SEE CUSTOMER STORIES" is softer than industry norm.
- Copy is fluffy in places: "Fuel Fearless Growth," "Dare to grow differently" — vague brand-voice headlines without specifics.
- Metrics use asterisked footnotes that aren't visible inline (erodes trust).
- No transparent pricing, no integrations strip, no compliance/security badges visible on homepage.
- The handwritten-script "Grow" word sacrifices legibility for vibe.
