01 — ORDER FRAUD PREVENTION

Score every order and route it to the right action automatically.

Most orders are legitimate. Our scoring engine approves them automatically — and flags the ones that aren't. High-risk orders go to a review queue; low-risk orders sail through. You control the thresholds. False positives go down; fraud stops before fulfilment.

  • Approve more legitimate orders by reducing false positives
  • Block card testing and bot traffic before cards are validated
  • Route high-risk orders to review — not blind declines
  • Real-time risk scoring based on device, behavior, and payment data
  • Flexible rules by order value, geography, and payment method

02 — CHARGEBACK MANAGEMENT

Dispute chargebacks with complete evidence, automatically compiled.

Friendly fraud — where customers dispute legitimate orders — is a growing problem in e-commerce. We log the transaction data and behavioural signals you need to fight these disputes: delivery confirmation, device fingerprint, purchase history, and communication records. All compiled into a submission-ready evidence package.

  • Automatic evidence package generation per disputed order
  • Logs delivery data, device fingerprint, and order history
  • Chargeback rate tracking by reason code and time period
  • Submission support for Visa and Mastercard dispute processes
