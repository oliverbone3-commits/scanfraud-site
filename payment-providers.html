Payment Providers

Add fraud protection to your merchant stack.

PSPs and payment aggregators need fraud screening across multiple merchants with different risk profiles. We give you a single platform to monitor all merchants, optimise routing, and manage disputes — deployable via API or as a white-label solution.

01 — MERCHANT FRAUD MONITORING

Monitor fraud rates across your entire merchant portfolio.

A single rogue merchant can put your acquiring relationship at risk. We give you a consolidated view of fraud rates, chargeback levels, and transaction volumes across all merchants — with automatic alerts when thresholds are breached. You can act before the problem reaches your processor.

  • Per-merchant fraud rate and chargeback monitoring in real time
  • Automatic alerts when fraud% exceeds configurable thresholds
  • Transaction-level drill-down to identify fraud patterns per merchant
  • Rule sets configurable per merchant, category, or MCC

02 — ROUTING OPTIMISATION & API

Optimise routing per merchant and deploy your way.

Different merchants need different routing logic — by card type, geography, or transaction size. Our routing engine lets you define rules per merchant or merchant group, with automatic cascade to backup providers on failure. The full platform is available via REST API or as a white-label deployment.

  • Merchant-level routing rules by card type, country, and amount
  • Automatic cascade to backup providers on decline or timeout
  • REST API for seamless integration into your existing stack
  • White-label option — your brand, your dashboard, our engine
