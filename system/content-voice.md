# Content Voice Guide

## Tone

B2B professional. We talk to payment and risk professionals — people who know the domain. No selling to beginners.

## Rules

1. **Concrete over abstract** — "decision in under 300ms" not "lightning-fast decisions"
2. **Specific over vague** — "100+ pre-built rules" not "comprehensive rule library"
3. **Benefit over feature** — "keep chargeback rates within thresholds" not "chargeback monitoring system"
4. **Active voice** — "We analyse transactions" not "Transactions are analysed"
5. **Short sentences** — max 20-25 words. If longer, split.

## Banned Words & Phrases

Never use on the site:
- revolutionary, cutting-edge, game-changing, next-generation
- seamlessly, effortlessly, simply (as filler)
- best-in-class, world-class, industry-leading
- leverage (as verb), synergy, paradigm
- "in today's fast-paced world"
- "trusted by thousands"
- any superlative without data backing it

## Patterns

### Headlines (H1)
- State the value directly: "See every transaction. Stop fraud in real time."
- Max 10 words. No questions. No exclamation marks.

### Subtitles
- Expand on H1 with mechanics: what the product does, how it helps.
- 1-2 sentences, max 30 words total.

### Card Descriptions
- Start with action verb: "Analyse...", "Route...", "Manage..."
- 1-2 sentences. Concrete outcome.

### Eyebrows
- Category / breadcrumb format: "Products / Transaction Monitoring"
- All uppercase, 10px, Inter font

### CTA
- "Book a demo" (primary)
- "Contact us" (secondary)
- Never "Get started free" or "Try now"

## Language

- British English spelling (analyse, organise, colour — except in code)
- Site content: English only
- Internal communication: Russian
